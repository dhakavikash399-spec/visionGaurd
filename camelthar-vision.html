01 — CORE

VisionGuardMate — Find a Travel Companion

The feature nobody built for India

Solo traveler in Jaisalmer tomorrow? Post your plan — fort, time, vibe. Others going the same place join you. Real-time, verified, personality-matched. No group tours. No awkward solo moments. Just two strangers becoming travel memories.

🔥 Highest Priority

02

🎭

Character Trails

Same Rajasthan. Different You.

Choose who you want to be — The Warrior, The Artisan, The Nomad, The Royal. Each character unlocks a completely different Rajasthan experience. Organized by identity, not just geography.

🧭 Unique Experience Layer

03

Thar Passport

Your digital travel identity

Every trip earns stamps. Every story earns points. Rise from Desert Wanderer to Thar Legend. Your passport becomes a shareable proof of real Rajasthan experiences — not just Instagram photos.

Ask a Local

Living knowledge, not dead articles

Verified locals — guides, craftsmen, chai shop owners, auto drivers — answer real traveler questions in 24hrs. Every answer becomes permanent content on that destination page. Pages grow smarter every day.

📚 Content Flywheel

Live from Rajasthan

What's happening right now

"Jaisalmer fort at 6am — completely empty." Real-time micro-updates from current travelers. The only travel platform where information is hours old, not months old. Solves the biggest pain in travel planning.

Phase 2 — Month 1–2 · Simple Feature Build

Launch VisionGuardMate + Live Feed

Basic profiles with travel personality tags Post & join plan feature — in-app or WhatsApp linked Live from Rajasthan micro-update feed First affiliate: local jeep/guide bookings via VisionGuardMate groups

Phase 3 — Month 3–4 · Experience Layer

Character Trails + Ask a Local

Build one full trail: "The Artisan" in Jaipur Recruit 5 verified locals per city for Ask a Local Trail diary content format for writers Affiliate: craft cooperatives, food tours, heritage stays
